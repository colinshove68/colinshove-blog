This is the first post. A few words on what this site is for.

## What this is

A writing blog. Not a service site, not a portfolio, not a funnel. The plan
is for it to hold the thinking that does not fit on the Frively site: longer
essays on web performance, commentary on the local web industry, the occasional
tutorial, and personal writing when I feel like it.

## How it is built

Astro, deployed to Cloudflare Pages, with posts written in MDX. No CMS, no
database, no plugins. Static HTML, zero JavaScript by default. The whole site
weighs less than a single hero image on the average WordPress homepage.

That is not a virtue signal. It is the standard I hold every site I build to,
because lots of small businesses get sold sites that fail this bar and never
know. Worth practising what I preach.

## Subscribing

The simplest way to follow along is the <a href="/rss.xml">RSS feed</a>. No
newsletter, no popups, no tracking. If you want to read what I write, you can.

More to come.
