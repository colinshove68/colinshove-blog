This is the first post. A short note on what this blog is for.

## What this is

Simple ideas to make small to medium sized businesses perform better.
That is the brief in a single sentence. Each post here will pick one
practical idea, explain it clearly, and leave you with something you
can actually do.

## Who it is for

The writing is aimed at four kinds of business:

- **Local businesses** running shops, studios, or services in their
  town.
- **Professional services** like solicitors, accountants, consultants,
  and coaches.
- **Trades** including builders, electricians, plumbers, and gardeners.
- **Salons** including hair, beauty, barber, and nails.

Use the filter at the top of the homepage to narrow posts down to your
kind of business.

## What to expect

Short pieces. Concrete advice. No fluff, no jargon, no twenty-paragraph
preambles before the actual point. If a post has a free download
attached, you will see it at the bottom of the article.

## Subscribing

The simplest way to follow along is the email signup at the bottom of
this page, or the <a href="/rss.xml">RSS feed</a>. No popups, no
tracking. If you want to read what I write, you can.

More to come.
